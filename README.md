## Online Cake Sales Store Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/3afb400d6b2b4d3996e50fac54bab5ad/ghb20250917) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used to run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Products
4. Forum
5. Announcement Information
6. Personal Center
7. Delivery Address
8. Product Collection
9. Product Reviews
10. Product Orders
11. Shopping Cart



**Staff Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Product Management
6. Product Review Management
7. Product Collection Management
8. Product Order Management
9. Forum Management
10. Announcement Information Management



**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (change password, personal information)
3. Administrator Management
4. Staff Management
5. User Management
6. Product Management
7. Product Review Management
8. Product Collection Management
9. Product Order Management
10. Basic Data Management (product type management, staff type management, member level type management, announcement type management)
11. Forum Management
12. Announcement Information Management
13. Carousel Image Management
